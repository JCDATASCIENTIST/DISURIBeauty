The hydration foundation with collagen signal. Triple Collagen Firming Essence is the lightweight base that preps skin for creams while delivering collagen-boosting actives.

Size: 150ml · Made in Korea

Key ingredients: Triple Collagen Complex (1,500ppm) — collagen signal + light hydration · Hyaluronic Acid (1%) — hydration foundation · Adenosine (0.5%) — circulation + collagen boost · Niacinamide (1.5%) — pore refining + barrier support

How to use

  • After toner, before cream
  • Dispense 2–3 drops into palm
  • Pat gently into face + neck
  • Let absorb (30 seconds)
  • Follow with cream

Helps improve the appearance of hydration, smoothness, and firmness with continued use. A cosmetic essence, not a treatment for any medical condition.
