## Full Onboarding Package Components

When generating a full onboarding package, create ALL of the following:

### 1. Welcome Sequence (3 messages)

**Message 1: Welcome (Day 0)**
- Warm, empowering welcome to the Queens community
- Confirm their tier and what they'll receive
- Set expectations for the first 30 days
- Tone: Excited but professional — "the friend who's genuinely thrilled you're here"

**Message 2: Toolkit Delivery (Day 1-2)**
- Deliver access to AI Prompt Library, Content Calendar Template, Caption + Hashtag Bank
- Quick-start instructions for creating their first post
- Link to Queen Academy Module 1
- Tone: Helpful and action-oriented

**Message 3: First Check-In (Day 7)**
- Ask how their first week went
- Offer help with any toolkit questions
- Encourage posting their first content piece
- Tone: Supportive without being pushy

### 2. Queen Academy Module 1: Brand Foundation

Generate training content covering:

**Section A: The DISURI Story**
- Brand origin (founder story — told as "our founder Joel" not as personal narrative)
- Mission: Empowering urban women through Korean skincare science
- Tagline: "Glow Like You Own The City"
- Brand pillars: Urban Luxe, Cultural Empowerment, Science Meets Soul, Community Movement

**Section B: Brand Voice Rules**
- The "We Are / We Are Not" identity table
- Five voice attributes: Warm Authority, Education-First, Radically Transparent, Culturally Fluent, Empowerment-Driven
- Compliance essentials: appearance-based claims only
- Anti-MLM language rules
- Required disclosures (#DISURIQueen or #DISURIPartner)

**Section C: Product Knowledge Essentials**
- Quick reference for all 7 SKUs + 3 bundles
- Key ingredients and what they do (appearance-based language only)
- Who each product is best for
- How to build routines and recommend bundles

**Section D: The 7-Question Pre-Publish Checklist**
- Walk through each question with examples
- Common violations and how to fix them
- Emphasize: "All 7 = Yes? Publish with confidence."

### 3. AI Marketing Toolkit Setup Guide

Generate a step-by-step setup document:

**For Digital Queens:**
- How to access and use the AI Prompt Library (ChatGPT/Claude prompts)
- Setting up their affiliate code and tracking link
- Content Calendar Template walkthrough
- Caption + Hashtag Bank introduction
- First assignment: Create 3 posts using the prompt library

**For Pro Queens:**
- Everything above, plus:
- Wholesale ordering process
- In-person consultation framework ("glow consultation")
- Professional marketing materials (shelf talkers, brochures)
- Client follow-up sequence templates

**For Boutique Queens:**
- Everything above, plus:
- Retail display setup guide
- Wholesale reorder process
- Staff training mini-guide (so boutique employees can sell DISURI)

### 4. First 30-Day Content Calendar

Generate a week-by-week calendar following this structure:

| Week | Focus | Content Types | Goals |
|------|-------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | Introduction + Brand Story | 2-3 intro posts, 1 product spotlight | Establish presence, announce partnership |
| 2 | Product Deep-Dives | 3 educational posts across 2 products | Build credibility through ingredient knowledge |
| 3 | AI Content Creation | 5 posts using prompt library (post 3) | Demonstrate toolkit value, build rhythm |
| 4 | Sales + Optimization | 3 posts + 2 stories/DMs | First sales, testimonial collection |

Each week includes:
- Specific content pillar assignments (Barrier Science, K-Beauty Authority, Urban Skin Survival, etc.)
- Product rotation across the line
- Platform recommendations (which content goes where)
- AI prompt suggestions for each post

### 5. Compliance Training Quick-Start

A condensed version of the Queen Content Compliance Framework:
- The 7-question checklist on one page
- Top 5 most common violations with before/after examples
- "Never Say / Always Say" terminology card
- Required disclosure language and placement rules
- Where to get help if unsure about a claim

## Voice Rules for Onboarding Content

All onboarding content must follow these rules:

- **Queens speak as brand partners, not as the brand founder** — they say "our founder Joel built DISURI after..." not "I built DISURI..."
- **Education-first framing** — every training module should teach, not just instruct
- **Empowerment without hype** — "Build your beauty business" not "Achieve financial freedom"
- **Anti-MLM clarity** — always emphasize product sales, never recruitment
- **Bilingual consideration** — provide Spanish translations where applicable
- **Compliance language baked in** — all product references use appearance-based claims
